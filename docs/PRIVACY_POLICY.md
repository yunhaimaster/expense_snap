# Privacy Policy for Expense Snap

**Last Updated: January 7, 2026**

## Overview

Expense Snap ("the App") is an expense tracking application designed for employees to record business expenses. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

## Information Collection and Use

### Data Stored Locally

The App stores the following data **locally on your device only**:

- **Expense records**: Description, amount, date, currency, and category
- **Receipt images**: Photos you take of receipts
- **User preferences**: Your name, preferred currency, and app settings

This data is stored in your device's private storage and is not transmitted to our servers.

### Camera Permission

The App requests camera access to:
- Take photos of receipts for expense documentation
- Scan receipts using OCR (Optical Character Recognition) to auto-fill expense details

Photos are stored locally on your device and are never uploaded to external servers unless you explicitly choose to back up to Google Drive.

### Internet Access

The App uses internet access for:
- **Exchange rate updates**: Fetching current currency exchange rates from public APIs
- **Google Drive backup** (optional): If you choose to enable cloud backup, your expense data and receipt images are uploaded to your personal Google Drive account

### Google Sign-In

If you choose to use the Google Drive backup feature:
- We use Google Sign-In to authenticate your Google account
- We only request access to app-specific storage in your Google Drive
- We do not access any other data in your Google account

## Data Storage

- All expense data and receipt images are stored locally on your device
- Cloud backup to Google Drive is **optional** and requires your explicit consent
- Data backed up to Google Drive is stored in your personal account and governed by Google's privacy policy

## Data Sharing

We do **not**:
- Sell your personal information
- Share your data with third parties
- Collect analytics or tracking data
- Store your data on our servers

## Data Security

- Local data is stored in the app's private directory, inaccessible to other apps
- OAuth tokens for Google Drive are stored using secure encrypted storage
- Receipt images are compressed and stored privately

## Data Deletion

You can delete your data at any time:
- Delete individual expenses and receipts within the app
- Clear all data from the app settings
- Uninstall the app to remove all local data
- Delete backup data from your Google Drive manually

## Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last Updated" date.
